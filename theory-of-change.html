THEORY OF CHANGE

EMIT's Theory of Change describes the change that we wish to see in the world and our understanding of how we will contribute to that change. We describe our activities and programs (i.e. our interventions) and relate these logically to the desired immediate, mid-term and long-term changes.

Theory

Improved quality of life for those negatively affected by leadership failures in society.

01

Activities

  • Enable reporting of impact stories
  • Facilitate stakeholder networking and support
  • Nurture community transformation programs
  • Encourage socio-economic transactions and collaboration
  • Train and develop leaders Recruit leadership teams
02

Short Term Outcomes

EquippedLeaders

  • Equipped with pertinent skills and knowledge
  • Recruited teams who share their vision
  • Connected with other like-minded leaders
  • Focused life-purpose
  • Motivated and encouraged to bring change
03

Medium Term Outcomes

CommunityProjects

  • Teams are actively addressing community needs by founding programs that are generous, compassionate and enterprising
  • They inspire and facilitate systemic change
04

Long Term Outcomes

Transformed Communities

  • Communities are healthy, thriving with hope for the future
  • Take ownership of their own well-being
  • Responding to challenges with vision and determination
05

Impact

Improved quality of life for those negatively affected by leadership failures in society.

IMPACT
